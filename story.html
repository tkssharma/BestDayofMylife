How We Met

It took more than just a few meetings for us. In fact, we will always remember our first meeting at Dunkin, WTP jaipur.
- - Not because it was a good meeting (because it wasn't);
- - Not even because we liked each other right away (because we didn't);
But because we are still amazed by how things started and how have things turned out. We fight and we get sad. We fight and we make-up. We ignore and we miss. We ignore and we call. We get angry and we don't talk. We get angry and we talk. We always trust in our bond to take us through anything that life can throw at us. To that end, we are embarking on the journey to make our relationship into something really beautiful.

Please join us for the opportunity to see real magic happen.
